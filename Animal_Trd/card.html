Siberian Husky

Siberian Huskies have captured the imagination of the masses, as well as artists, writers, and film producers, with their eye-catching looks and awe-inspiring talents.

  • Dog Breed Group: Working Dogs
  • Height: 8 to 11 inches
  • Weight: 35 to 60 pounds
  • Life Span: 12 to 15 years

Siberian Huskies are very curious and very active. They can be very destructive both inside and out. If they are left uncrated inside, the breed can destroy a house and cause a wide variety of damage. They are also known as an "escape artists" and have been known to wander away and disappear. They can jump fences, break tie-out chains, slip collars and find any other way to escape.
While they enjoy howling, they rarely bark and they will not alert you and bark if someone comes onto your property.
